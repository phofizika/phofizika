For Students, By Students

Our Mission

In Singapore, many schools do not provide a structured Olympiad curriculum to prepare their students, saturating the National teams with students from select few schools only. FIZIKA was founded by a group of 3 JC2 students from 3 vastly different backgrounds. Our team's mission is to make olympiad education free, accessible, and effective for all students in Singapore, preparing them for the highest level of Physics Olympiad competitions such as the IPhO, APhO, INSO, & SPhO. We hope that this project also serves as a community for people to discuss all things Physics, to share and learn from each other. Every student has immense potential, and should not be limited by their resources.

Lesson Syllabus & Schedule

This 4-month course comprehensively covers the Singapore Physics Olympiad (SPhO) syllabus. While we plan to offer multiple classes eventually, we are launching SPhO training first.

SPhO Training Class

Schedule: Saturdays, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

This class will systematically go through the topics listed below. Detailed weekly schedules will be provided to registered participants.

Mechanics

  • Kinematics
  • Statics
  • Dynamics
  • Oscillations
  • Rotation
  • Gravitation
  • Fluid Mechanics

Electromagnetism (EM)

  • Electrostatics
  • Electric Current, DC Circuits
  • Magnetostatics
  • Lorentz Force
  • Electromagnetic Induction (EMI)
  • AC/RLC Circuits

Thermodynamics

  • Ideal Gases, Thermodynamics Laws
  • Methods of Heat Transfer
  • Statistical Mechanics

Waves & Optics

  • Wave Equation & Standing Waves
  • Interference & Diffraction
  • Geometrical Optics

Relativity

  • Kinematics
  • Dynamics
