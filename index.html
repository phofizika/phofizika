Typically in May

↓

Year 5 Students

Lesson Syllabus & Schedule

This 4-month course comprehensively covers the Singapore Physics Olympiad (SPhO) syllabus. While we plan to offer multiple classes eventually, we are launching SPhO training first.

SPhO Training Class

Schedule: Saturdays, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

This class will systematically go through the topics listed below. Detailed weekly schedules will be provided to registered participants.

Mechanics

  • Kinematics
  • Statics
  • Dynamics
  • Oscillations
  • Rotation
  • Gravitation
  • Fluid Mechanics

Electromagnetism (EM)

  • Electrostatics
  • Electric Current, DC Circuits
  • Magnetostatics
  • Lorentz Force
  • Electromagnetic Induction (EMI)
  • AC/RLC Circuits

Thermodynamics

  • Ideal Gases, Thermodynamics Laws
  • Methods of Heat Transfer
  • Statistical Mechanics

Waves & Optics

  • Wave Equation & Standing Waves
  • Interference & Diffraction
  • Geometrical Optics

Relativity

  • Kinematics
  • Dynamics
